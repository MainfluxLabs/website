About Us

Mainflux team has an extensive working experience and knowledge of hardware and intelligent devices and can provide operational expertise for the best cost-quality related solutions and support needed for every IoT project.

Founders

DRASKO DRASKOVIC
MAINFLUX CO-FOUNDER

Draško his Electronics Engineer with over 15 years of professional experience gained in fortune 500 companies as well as technological start-ups. He worked in telecom and semiconductor giants like NOKIA, Alcatel-Lucent or Texas Instruments as well as in early-stage companies like Sequans Communications and Devialet - being engaged in embedded systems, semiconductor and telecommunication technologies.

During these years, Draško gained valuable expertise in operating systems and device drivers, RT processing, software and hardware networking technologies, RF technolgies and wireless and mobile telecommunications, microcontrollers and microprocessors, FPGA, sensor networks, distributed systems, security and encryption techniques and many more.

Additionally, Draško earned his reputation in Open Source community by being constantly involved and contributing to projects dealing with low-level kernel programming and device drivers like OpenOCD JTAG debugger, OpenWRT Linux and similar.

He was one of the creators of WeIO board, an innovative open IoT HW based on MIPS Linux with complex Python firmware, which he lead from the design through sucessful crowdfunding campaing to produiction and commercialization. Project won many international prizes for innovation.

Draško is the initial author of Mainflux IoT Platform, an open-sorce project on which he plays the role of BDFL. He is also a Technical Steering Comitee member on Linux Foundation's EdgeX Foundry project and an avid speaker on many technical conferences around the world.

Draško holds MSc degree in Electrical Engineering from Belgrade University.


JANKO ISIDOROVIC
